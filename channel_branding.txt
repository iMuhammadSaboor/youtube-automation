6. UPLOAD SCHEDULE RECOMMENDATION
================================================================================

Phase 1 (Months 1-3): 1 video per week (Saturday, 5:00 PM EST)
  - Focus on building a library of 12+ high-quality videos.
  - Consistency matters more than frequency at this stage.

Phase 2 (Months 4-8): 2 videos per week (Wednesday + Saturday, 5:00 PM EST)
  - Algorithm rewards higher frequency once quality is proven.
  - Use Wednesday for shorter format (8-12 min), Saturday for long form (15-25 min).

Phase 3 (Month 9+): 2-3 videos per week
  - Add a Shorts upload on Monday to capture mobile traffic.
  - Repurpose long-form highlights into Shorts.

Best posting times for mystery/discovery niche:
  - Saturday 5:00 PM EST (peak leisure browsing)
  - Wednesday 2:00 PM EST (midweek engagement spike)
  - Sunday 11:00 AM EST (weekend catch-up viewing)

7. BRANDING COLORS
================================================================================

Primary Palette:
  Deep Midnight Blue   #0B0F2E   (backgrounds, overlays)
  Royal Gold           #C9A84C   (titles, accents, highlights)
  Pure White           #FFFFFF   (body text, contrast elements)

Secondary Palette:
  Muted Silver         #8A8D9B   (subtitles, secondary text)
  Blood Crimson        #8B1A1A   (alert accents, mystery emphasis)
  Charcoal             #1C1C1E   (alternate dark background)

Usage Guide:
  - Thumbnails: Deep Midnight Blue background, Royal Gold title text,
    White subtitle text. Use Blood Crimson sparingly for emphasis.
  - Channel Banner: Gradient from Deep Midnight Blue to Charcoal,
    channel name in Royal Gold, tagline in Pure White.
  - Video Intros/Outros: Midnight Blue base with Gold animated accents.
  - Lower Thirds: Charcoal bar with White text and Gold accent line.

Font Recommendations:
  - Titles/Thumbnails: Bebas Neue or Oswald (bold, condensed, cinematic)
  - Body/Descriptions: Inter or Roboto (clean, readable)
